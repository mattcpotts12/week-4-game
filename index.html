How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four cyrstals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number (Number to match), you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidding from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
